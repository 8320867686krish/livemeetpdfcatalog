In Magento 2, whenever customers make no payments for orders, the order status is set to pending. Also if the store is enabled with offline payment methods, the store may have a large number of fake orders with pending status. Each time an order is placed, the quantity gets decreased. Now when the store has many orders with pending status, the product may get out of stock soon. Here, the best option is to cancel such orders. Meetanshi has developed Magento 2 Auto Cancel Order to automatically cancel orders with the pending and pending payment status.

Magento 2 Auto Cancel Order extension is a must-have when you face issues like customers abandoning the payments or payments abandoned due to stock unavailability, etc. Such situations create orders that are no longer useful and are to be canceled. Instead of manually doing the task, opt for the automatic cancelation feature offered by the module!
